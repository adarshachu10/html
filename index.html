Kerala tourism



Kerala is a state in India known for its beautiful beaches, lush green hills, and backwaters.






Kerala is surrounded by the Western Ghats that cradle majestic tea, coffee and spice plantations on one side. The other side is hemmed with a 600km Arabian sea shoreline. If you want to experience the best of both worlds, Kerala is the place for you.It is often called "God's own country" for its natural beauty and cultural heritage. Kerala is a popular tourist destination for both domestic and international travelers.
